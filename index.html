About Me

I previously worked as a Member of Technical Staff at Oracle India Pvt Ltd, Bangalore. I was part of the Oracle’s Digital Assistant team working on realtime analytics.

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi with a major in Electrical Engineering in 2021. I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Sandeep Kumar on my Bachelor’s Thesis titled Graph Model Selection in Gaussian Graphical Models. Previously, I interned with Oracle India in their Digital Assistant team in summer 2020. Before that, I worked with Prof. Arpan Chattopadhyay as a research intern in 2019.

I was an active member of the Robotics Club where we designed and developed drives (holonomic, mecanum etc), multi-legged locomotive robots, ROS-based differential drive etc. I have a strong inclination towards applications of AI for the betterment of society.
